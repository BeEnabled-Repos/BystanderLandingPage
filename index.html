Your information, ready when it matters most

Bystander by 119 is a free digital alert that lets someone nearby see your important information and get you the right help — even if you can't speak for yourself.

How It Works

  1. 1

    Set up your profile

    Add your important information to the app. It stays private and secure.

  2. 2

    Share your QR code

    The app gives you a QR code. In an emergency, someone nearby scans it with their phone.

  3. 3

    They see how to help you

    Your information tells them what you need so they can get you the right help.

Why Bystander by 119?

  • Free to use — no cost to you.
  • Works like a digital alert, right on your phone.
  • You control your information. You choose what to share.
  • Your information is private and secure.
  • Made by BeEnabled — a company that designs accessibility for everybody.
